How Do We Help?

Problem: Critical Gaps in Current Food Safety Controls

Most food production lines still rely on metal detectors or basic 2D X-ray systems. These technologies have fundamental limitations:

  • Non-metallic contaminants such as glass, stone, and plastic go undetected.
  • 2D systems often miss hazards when contaminants are oriented at certain angles.
  • Recalls cost an average of €50,000 per container — plus reputational damage that can take years to repair.

In short, your quality control process is vulnerable — and your brand is exposed.

Common Foreign Contaminants
Contaminant Source Example
Glass fragments Packaging breakage or transport shock
Metal shavings Equipment wear or processing failure
Stone particles Raw material residue (e.g. produce)
Plastic pieces Packaging materials or closures

Key Advantages:

Mito is not just an inspection tool — it’s a strategic safeguard for your brand and bottom line.
